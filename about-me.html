About Me

Hi my name is Erik Kantrowitz is a junior test engineer working in robotics with a background in software testing and a growing focus on frontend development. He originally set out to study propulsion engineering, but after being assigned to a rover project in his university’s rocket club, he quickly discovered a passion for robotics. That interest deepened through the robotics club, where he eventually led one of the main projects cementing his path toward building and improving complex systems.

Outside of work and learning, Erik is a musician who plays upbeat, driving indie music on drums and guitar. While it’s been a few years since he played in a band, he still jams at home. He’s also an avid reader and makes a point to spend time outdoors whenever possible.
